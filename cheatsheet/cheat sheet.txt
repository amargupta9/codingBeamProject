exTurn is a cloud-native engineering services organization that helps clients modernize and build software platforms. Specializing in digital transformation, NexTurn supports businesses through cloud-native solutions, data analytics, security, and enterprise cloud transformation. Their services are designed to drive continuous value creation for clients by enabling rapid innovation cycles and modern engineering practices, such as DevOps and cloud computing.

The company operates globally, with headquarters in Bellevue, Washington, and has a significant presence in Hyderabad, India. NexTurn partners with enterprises across industries, offering tailored cloud solutions, including cloud-native engineering and cloud data analytics to improve customer experiences and streamline business processes.
